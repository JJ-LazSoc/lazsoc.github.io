FAQ

Application processes for new Laz clubs start in April. Clubs will first apply by completing a question form detailing:

1. Vision for the club
2. Rough schedule of events for the year
3. Rough budget for the year
4. Stakeholder report outlining positions they are hiring for and why

The second part of the process will include an interview process. The overall process takes approximately a month to complete. Unfortunately, not all clubs will be accepted to avoid overlap so applicants should ensure that their new club has a unique value proposition!

Check under our "Internal Events" page!

Contact technology@lazsoc.ca for any inquiries about the website!

Clubs typically hire during the beginning of the semester. Check under "Hiring" to see what positions need to be filled!

Join clubs and sign up for events!

Applying for director or executive positions are great ways to get involved in Laz. We encourage you to take advantage of our general memberships and use them to learn more about the specific industries that interest you!

Events are also great ways to learn more about certain fields and network with like-minded individuals.
